Egg-Fried Noodles

Ingredients

  • mie noodles
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 50 ml water
  • 3 carrots
  • 1 leek
  • 3 spring onions
  • 4 tbsp sunflower oil

Instructions

  1. Cook the noodles in plenty of water according to the packet instructions, drain and rinse with cold water.
  2. Break the eggs into a bowl and mix with 1/2 tsp garlic pepper. Mix the other 1/2 tsp garlic pepper with soy sauce and water in a small bowl.
  3. Peel the carrots, cut into thin slices, then into very thin strips. Slice leek and spring onions thinly. Wash and clean them well.
  4. Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a wok pan, add the egg mixture and cook for a minute while stirring. Remove from pan and set aside.
  5. Pour the remaining oil into the pan and add the noodles. Stir-fry for 2 minutes. Add the vegetables, deglaze with soy sauce and stir well.
  6. Add the eggs and spring onions and mix well.
